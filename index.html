About Wasa Animal Rescue Team

Wasa Animal Rescue Team in Amravati is a dedicated organization focused on rescuing and rehabilitating animals in need. Our mission is to provide shelter, medical care, and a second chance to animals who are abandoned, injured, or at risk. Our passionate team works tirelessly, often in difficult conditions, to save as many lives as possible and create a safer environment for all animals.

Founded in 2013, we have rescued over many animals, ranging from domestic pets to wildlife, and are committed to educating the community on animal welfare and responsible pet ownership. At Wasa, every life matters, and we continue to strive for a better tomorrow for all living creatures.
